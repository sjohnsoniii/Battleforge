Battle Forge

Strategy // Simulation // Systems

Battle Forge is an experimental platform for building and testing strategic systems in real time. It sits at the intersection of game theory, simulation, and modern web technology—designed to explore how decisions, probabilities, and structured logic play out under pressure.

At its core, Battle Forge is about turning abstract strategy into something interactive and measurable. Whether it's modeling competitive scenarios, simulating outcomes, or creating tools that sharpen decision-making, the goal is the same: take ideas that normally live in spreadsheets or theory and bring them to life in a way that can be tested, iterated, and refined.

This is not just a game, and it's not just a tool. It's a framework for experimentation. A sandbox where systems can be built, broken, and rebuilt quickly. Where intuition meets data, and strategy evolves through iteration.

As the project evolves, Battle Forge will expand into more advanced simulations, real-time feedback systems, and AI-assisted decision modeling. The long-term vision is to create a platform that can support everything from competitive strategy testing to practical, real-world applications in analysis and planning.

Right now, you're looking at the foundation. What comes next is where things start to get interesting.
